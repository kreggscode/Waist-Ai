TL;DR: We respect your privacy. Your health data stays on your device. We don't sell or share your personal information. Period.

1. Introduction

Welcome to WaistHip ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal health information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Measurement Data: Waist and hip measurements you manually enter
  • Meal Information: Photos of food and nutritional data
  • Health Metrics: Waist-to-hip ratio calculations and progress tracking

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns (stored locally)
  • Camera Access: Only when you use the food scanning feature

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
  • Calculate your waist-to-hip ratio and health insights
  • Process food images using AI for nutritional analysis
  • Generate personalized health recommendations
  • Improve app performance and user experience
Important: All your health data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload your measurements or personal health information to our servers.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Local Storage

Your measurement history, meal logs, and personal data are stored exclusively on your device using encrypted local databases. This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it
  • We cannot access your personal health information
  • If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted

4.2 AI Processing

When you use the food scanning feature:

  • Food images are sent to our AI service provider for analysis
  • Images are processed in real-time and not stored permanently
  • No personally identifiable information is attached to images
  • AI providers are GDPR and CCPA compliant

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • AI Vision API: For food recognition and nutritional analysis
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix app crashes (no personal data included)

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all your stored data within the app
  • Delete: Remove individual measurements or all data
  • Export: Export your data for personal use
  • Opt-out: Disable AI features and use manual entry only

7. Children's Privacy

WaistHip is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes

9. International Users

WaistHip is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (European Union)
  • CCPA (California, USA)
  • HIPAA principles (USA healthcare)

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@waisthip.app
  • Support: support@waisthip.app
Your Trust Matters: We built WaistHip with privacy as a core principle. Your health data is sensitive, and we treat it with the utmost respect and security.
